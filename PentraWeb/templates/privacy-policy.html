Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PentraWeb collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

Your data is used for the following purposes:

We take data security seriously and implement various measures to protect your information, including:

User Rights
